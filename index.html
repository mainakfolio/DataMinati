    About DataMinati

    In the fast paced world, Data is superpower. But to know and be able to use this data in a proper systematic manner is not always as easy as it might sound. AI Models must be able to run in web browsers to display knowledge to the intended communities. Also, the speed of processing and deriving results must be increased to a lightning fast speed, to survive the competition. Hence, to solve these problems DataMinati comes to light. With our idea to establish a "New Data Order", DataMinati will provide it's esteemed users the previlage to learn for free, apply the learned skills, (for free) and showcase their assets to the world(also, for free). Recuiters searching for experienced and valued members can find their match too. Thus, generating an overall atmosphere of Data Science, Data Analytics, Business Analytics, Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence. So, are you ready to be the Datafer (Data Lucifer)?
